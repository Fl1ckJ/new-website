Discover & Define

Every implementation begins with understanding the project, the asset, and the challenges to solve. Through a kick-off workshop and site visits SteelTrace aligns with the client on scope, stakeholders, quality requirements, and expected outcomes, then analyses and maps how project information is captured, reviewed, approved, and reported across the lifecycle.

What we establish
Project scope & outcomes Key stakeholders & contacts Quality documentation & standards Traceability requirements Existing software & data sources Inspection & testing workflows Approval & reporting obligations Complete workflow map

The result is a clearly defined project with measurable value and a workflow blueprint for configuration — for most clients a one-time onboarding activity that can be reused for future projects.

Configure & Connect

SteelTrace stands up the project environment and onboards every participating organization with role-based access. Inspection & Test Plans, Quality Control Plans, specifications, and acceptance criteria are digitized into the platform, data flows are connected, and approval workflows are configured so every record is validated and routed automatically.

Configured & connected
Project domain & supplier onboarding Role-based permissions & approval rights ITPs, QCPs & acceptance criteria Direct entry · bulk upload · API ERP / MES / LIMS / NDT integrations Unique Pipe & Weld IDs Approval workflows & review stages

As data is submitted it is automatically validated against requirements — surfacing missing information and non-conformities early — with every review, approval, and change captured in a complete digital audit trail.

Execute & Hand Over

After a pilot trial and user training, SteelTrace becomes the central platform for quality, inspection, and traceability data — checking every entry in real time and continuously building a living Digital Asset Record. At completion, handover deliverables are generated automatically, and the data carries forward as the foundation for the entire asset lifecycle.
