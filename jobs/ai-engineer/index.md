> Build AI on data you can actually trust. This is the role where the mission and the work meet most directly.

You'll work on verification, extraction and intelligence over structured manufacturing data, on a product whose whole purpose is trustworthy data and resisting AI-falsified records.

### What you'll do
- Build extraction and verification pipelines over certificates, inspection reports and manufacturing data
- Apply LLMs to a domain where correctness and provenance matter more than fluency
- Work closely with product and customers to turn model output into something operators can rely on

### Must-haves
- Experience with LLM-based systems and data extraction
- Comfortable with our stack: JavaScript / Node.js, MongoDB, and modern AI tooling (Claude, Cursor, Gemini, Codex)
- Based in or willing to relocate to South Limburg, NL
