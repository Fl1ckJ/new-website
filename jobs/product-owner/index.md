> Own what we build and why. Translate customer reality across operators, EPCs and manufacturers into a clear product roadmap.

You'll work between engineering, customers and commercial to ship things that matter. This is a senior, high-ownership role: you'll set priorities directly with our tier-1 customers, not through layers of process.

### What you'll do
- Translate operator, EPC and manufacturer needs into a clear, prioritized roadmap
- Work daily with engineering to ship, and with customers to validate
- Own outcomes end-to-end, from problem definition to shipped feature

### Must-haves
- B2B / enterprise SaaS product experience
- Comfort working in complex technical domains
- Based in or willing to relocate to South Limburg, NL
