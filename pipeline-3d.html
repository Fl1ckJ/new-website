Across the energy supply chain

Five segments. One Smart Manufacturing Record.
Segment 01 — OP

Pipeline Operators (IOCs & NOCs)

Who

Asset owners building onshore, offshore, LNG, hydrogen and CCS infrastructure — the market makers that define the rules.

Value

Compliance-by-design, blockchain-verified records and faster project-to-operations handover.

Outcome

Trusted lifecycle data for integrity, AI and Digital Twins.

Segment 02 — EPC

EPC Contractors

Who

Engineering, Procurement and Construction contractors coordinating pipeline construction from spoolbase to offshore vessel.

Value

Automated digital MRBs generated during production, with real-time supplier quality visibility.

Outcome

Less QA/QC effort, cleaner deliverables, improved operator transparency.

Segment 03 — MFG

Manufacturers & Material Suppliers

Who

Steel mills, pipe makers, coating and clad companies generating critical production data.

Value

A data layer above MES — standardized exchange and melt-to-end-product traceability.

Outcome

One channel across all operator customers.

Segment 04 — WLD

Welding Contractors

Who

Pipeline welders and fabricators running welding-data platforms, automated inspection and qualification workflows.

Value

Weld parameters and inspections flow into the SMR automatically — one exchange channel for all clients.

Outcome

Documentation workload replaced by data exchange.

Segment 05 — NDT

Inspection & Testing Companies

Who

NDT, AUT, destructive and non-destructive testing labs verifying materials, welds and coatings.

Value

Inspection results integrated directly into the SMR — digitally verified, immutable approvals.

Outcome

Stronger compliance and audit readiness.
