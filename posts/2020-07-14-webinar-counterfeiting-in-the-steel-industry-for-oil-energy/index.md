SteelTrace is hosting another webinar, this time we touch on the topic of Counterfeiting in the Steel industry.

Together with our guests [Fergus Mason](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fergus-mason-9a9566136/), Linepipe Manager – Middle East at [Marubeni Itochu](https://www.benichu.com/en/) Steel Inc and [Mark Walsh CQP MCQI](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mark-walsh-cqp-mcqi-b09760b/) Technical Director of [Q-Sys Solutions](https://qsys-solutions.com/) we will touch upon the various topics surrounding counterfeit material and its consequences.

You might also like to read our blog post [5 ways to find out if an MTC is fake](post.html?slug=5-ways-to-find-out-if-an-mtc-material-test-certificate-is-fake) where give 5 tips for quality managers on how to spot a fake certificate.
