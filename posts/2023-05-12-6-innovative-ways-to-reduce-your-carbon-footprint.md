Steel is energy-intensive to produce, but the processes around it can become far more sustainable. A few ways digital traceability helps:

- No more printing, signing, stamping and scanning paper certificates
- Cradle-to-grave traceability for every product and property
- Early detection of non-conformities, so less material is wasted
- Smarter recycling by identifying each piece of steel and its properties
- Automated verification that removes slow, error-prone manual checks
- A path toward putting carbon data directly on certificates

Cutting waste and rework is good for the planet — and for the project.
