In business, data standardization carries real value — and that's especially true for steel in oil & gas. Four reasons it matters:

- **Inter-company data exchange** — connect once to a platform, exchange with everyone on it
- **Digital twins** — structured data is what models can actually consume
- **Accessibility** — find any parameter in seconds, not days
- **Automation** — consistent formats make rule-based checks possible

Standardisation is the unglamorous foundation everything else is built on.
