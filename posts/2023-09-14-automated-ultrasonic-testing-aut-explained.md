Automated Ultrasonic Testing (AUT) is a non-destructive technique that uses ultrasonic waves to inspect materials and detect flaws.

## AUT vs manual UT
AUT uses the same core components as manual UT — a transducer and visualisation equipment — but the scanner moves the probes automatically. With manual UT the inspector is in control; with AUT, speed and data recording are controlled by the equipment, removing a source of human variability.

For a digital record, that consistency matters: the parameters and results arrive as structured data that can be verified automatically against the procedure.
