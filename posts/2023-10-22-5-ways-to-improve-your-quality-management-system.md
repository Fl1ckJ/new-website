Do you recognise inefficient quality-management practices in your supply chain — and do you know how they affect your processes and products? Here are five places to start:

- Replace manual certificate checking with automated, rule-based validation
- Standardise the data format you accept from every supplier
- Capture data at the source instead of re-keying it later
- Make non-conformities visible the moment they occur
- Keep an immutable, auditable trail of every approval

Small changes here compound quickly across a project.
