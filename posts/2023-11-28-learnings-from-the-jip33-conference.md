In October 2023 we travelled to Norway for the JIP33 conference. Here are our five main takeaways:

- **Start in FEED.** To use the JIP-33 standard well, align all parties from the design phase.
- **Make adoption a requirement.** Trying something new is a risk; high-level buy-in turns it from optional to expected.
- **Involve suppliers early.** The earlier suppliers are aligned, the smoother execution becomes.
- **Standardisation drives down cost at scale.** When everyone knows what to expect, manufacturers can price more sharply.
- **"Gold-plated" requirements can still be cheaper** when applied consistently across many orders.
