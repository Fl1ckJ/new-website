EPCs sit at the centre of complex projects and are responsible for delivering sound solutions on time and on spec. SteelTrace supports that mandate end-to-end:

- A single inbox for every supplier's quality data
- Real-time verification against the QCP / ITP
- Digital MRBs generated during production, not after
- Early detection of non-conformities
- Full traceability from material to as-built
- Standardised data from every tier
- Less manual review effort for QA/QC teams
- Cleaner, faster handover to the operator
- Blockchain-secured, auditable approvals
- A trusted basis for integrity and lifecycle data
