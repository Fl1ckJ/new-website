Manufacturers face pressure on cost, lead time and quality all at once. A digital, data-native record helps on several fronts:

- One channel to deliver certified data to every customer
- Certificates generated straight from the MES
- Protection against counterfeiting of your products
- Automated traceability across production and testing
- Fewer non-conformities, caught earlier
- Less time spent re-formatting data per order
- A standardised layout customers can trust
- Faster, cleaner handover packages
- A foundation for future digital requirements

The result: you spend less time defending your data and more time making product.
