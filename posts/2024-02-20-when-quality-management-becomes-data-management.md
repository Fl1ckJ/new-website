Quality management in oil & gas is on the verge of a big change. New technologies automate work once done by hand, while operators' ambitions — turnaround simulations, Digital Twins — demand that quality data behave like structured, queryable data.

## Three threads converging
- **Data capture** at the source, in real time
- **Quality management** against the QCP / ITP
- **Supply chain** collaboration across every tier

When these stop being separate activities, quality data stops being a stack of documents and becomes an asset you can actually compute on.
